Solar installations

If you want to make a positive contribution to the environment and for your wallet then you must seriously consider to invest in a solar panel installation. 

There are several ways to design a solar panel installation. You can go off grid with battery backup power during the night or you can go on grid, feeding the leftover energy  into the grid. You can also go on grid with a battery backup.  

How many solar panels will you need? We can advise and explain what the best solution will be for your specific situation.

Solar panels can easily last 25 to 30 years but the necessary inverters and batteries not. It is  therefore important to choose the right inverter and batteries for your specific situation.

Advantage:

A solar panel system pays itself back.

The sun comes up every morning for free.

There is no CO2 polution.

You have no trouble with load shedding or a power breakdown from Escom.

The prices of solar installations have came down in the past 15 years.

Electricity prices on the other hand increase over the years. Your solar installation does not.


Disadvantage:

You have to invest first.
